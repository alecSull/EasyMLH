Hi! If you're new to hackathons, or find it difficult or stressful to participate in one, you've found the right place.

This site's purpose is to provide resources and tricks to help alleviate some of the stress of hackathons.

We hope that first time hackers, and veterans, will find the resources here extremely useful. We have everythign from music to checklists to ideas to de-stressing ideas.

Thanks for stopping by! try to learn something, and always have fun.
