Get Organized!
We recommend using a version control tool, such as GitHub. In addition to allowing multiple people who may be in different locations to work on the same project, GitHub also allows you to create a classical kanban board to organize your project.

Additionally, it can be helpful to set realistic goals, and try to form a schedule. Hackathons can be extremely stressful, so do everything you can to alleviate this stress. Setting realistic goals that you can follow throughout the hackathon is an extremely helpful process to track your progress.
